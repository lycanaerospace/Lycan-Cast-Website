FAQ

Questions, answered.

What pilots usually ask before the first flight.

Which cameras and receivers work?
Any camera or video receiver that works as a standard USB video device — including popular digital FPV ground units, HDMI capture sticks, and webcams. Plug it in and Lycan Cast picks its best quality automatically.
Does it need an internet connection?
No — and it never can. Lycan Cast has no network access at all, enforced by Android itself. Everything works in airplane mode, in the field, anywhere.
Where are my recordings saved?
In your gallery by default, or any folder you choose — including SD cards. Your recordings are normal video files you can play, edit, and share with any app.
What do I need to run it?
An Android phone or tablet running Android 8.0 or newer, plus the cable that fits your camera — that's it. No account, no sign-up, no licence keys.
Is Lycan Cast free?
Yes. No ads, no subscriptions, no locked features. Lycan Aerospace builds precision tools for pilots — this one is on us.
